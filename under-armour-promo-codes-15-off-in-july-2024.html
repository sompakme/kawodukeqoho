Does Under Armour have free shipping?

You can get free shipping from Under Armour if your order is $99 or more. This standard free shipping will take 4-6 business days to deliver your order. For orders less than $99, shipping costs $8.

Does Under Armour offer express shipping?

Yes, Under Armour offers express shipping for $15 which will arrive within 2-3 business days. 

Can I sign up for the Under Armour newsletter?

Yes, you can sign up to the Under Armour newsletter using your email to stay in the loop about the latest information about new arrivals, product launches, Under Armour exclusives, as well as 15% off your first order. If you sign up for UA Rewards, you can also save points towards future purchases as well.

Does Under Armour have student discounts?

Yes, at Under Armour they have a 15% student discount. Simply verify your student status using UNiDAYS to enjoy your money off. 

Can I shop the Under Armour sale?

Yes, Under Armour has regular seasonal sales throughout the year which you can shop the outlet section. It's a great chance to make some savings of up to 50% off. You can also shop the sale during seasonal periods such as Christmas or Black Friday. 

What is the returns policy at Under Armour?

Under Armour has a 60-day returns policy in case you're not 100% satisfied with your purchase. All items must be returned in their original condition, original packaging, and with all tags intact, for you to be eligible for a full refund.

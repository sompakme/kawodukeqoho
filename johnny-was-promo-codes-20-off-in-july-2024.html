FAQs

Can I get free shipping at Johnny Was?

Yes, Johnny Was offers free shipping for orders of $50 or more. If your order costs less, standard shipping has a flat fee of $7. You can also check this page for the occasional free shipping coupon or savings equivalent.  

Does Johnny Was have email discounts?

Yes, sign up for the Johnny Was email newsletter to get 20% off your first order. You can sign up by going to the Johnny Was website and entering your email address when prompted.  

When are the sales at Johnny Was?

Johnny Was has sales throughout the year on its dedicated ‘Sale’ page. Generally, you can save 30-75% off with the sale section. The ‘Last Chance Sale’ is a smart place to go for the best discounts on end-of-season items. For discerning shoppers, Black Friday and Cyber Monday are also key dates to shop. 

Does Johnny Was price match items?

Johnny Was will price match your item if it has been reduced within 14 days of purchasing at full price. However, they do not price match to other third-party retailers or outlets. 

What is the return policy at Johnny Was?

Returns are accepted at Johnny Was within 30 days of receipt. Your order should be returned unworn, unwashed, and with any original tags still attached. Any items reduced to 50% off or more are considered ‘final sale’ and cannot be returned. It is the customer’s responsibility to pay for return shipping costs.

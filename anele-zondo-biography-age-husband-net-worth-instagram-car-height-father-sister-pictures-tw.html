Early Life

Anele Zondo was born in New Castle (KwaZulu Natal), South Africa on the 24th of September, 1994.

Her parents worked in the educational sector, her father, a principal, while her mother, a teacher.

Growing up, she worked as a receptionist, driver, delivery girl, and PA to Mazondo Food Services, a food service owned by her sister.

Education

Anele Zondo had her primary and secondary education in KwaZulu Natal.

She also majored in media studies and holds a national diploma from Boston Media House Johannesburg.

Career

Anele Zondo ‘s first role was in the television series, It’s Complicated, which was followed up with a cameo appearance in Saints and Sinners, a Mzansi Magic Drama.

In 2018, she played Andile‘s girlfriend in The River. She acted alongside Lunga Mofekeng. Other roles kept coming in, they weren’t big roles, but she still performed very well.

In 2019, Anele Zondo was announced host at One Mic on SABC (Africa’s biggest rap battle with over 3 million viewers), the show was among the top 15 most-watched shows in Africa and it helped set the pace of her career.

In 2020, she became a co-host in Massive Music as a media personality.

Presently, Anele Zondo is the co-host of Telkom Monate Vibes, according to Anele in an interview with drum, she said “The show allowed me to be myself and show how versatile I can be. I bring in the fashion element on the show which is also a strength“, for Anele the show is a dream come true.

Who are Jack Nicholson's kids?

THE ACTOR has found massive fame and success in Hollywood.

In addition to being an extremely well-known actor, Jack Nicholson is also a father.

Who are Jack Nicholson's kids?

Jack, 85, has six children with five different women.

He shares 59-year-old actress Jennifer Nicholson with his ex-wife, retired actress Sandra Knight.

Jennifer was born on September 13, 1963, in Los Angeles, California.

The New Jersey native welcomed his second child named Caleb Goddard on September 26, 1970.

Caleb, 52, was also born in Los Angeles. His mother is actress Susan Anspach, who worked alongside Jack in the 1970 film, Five Easy Pieces.

As reported by PEOPLE, Susan wed Mark Goddard during her pregnancy with Caleb. Mark then adopted the baby boy.

Then, 25 years after Caleb's birth, Jack denied being Caleb's biological father. However, Caleb spoke to People and said that Jack privately accepted him as his son.

Jack's daughter Honey Hollman was born on January 26, 1982.

As People notes, the 42-year-old grew up in Denmark, where she lived with her mother, model Winnie Hollman.

Jack then had two children with actress Rebecca Broussard.

On April 16, 1990, the pair welcomed their daughter, Lorraine Broussard Nicholson.

The 32-year-old was born in Los Angeles and has pursued an acting and directing career.

She played Alana Blanchard in the 2011 movie, Soul Surfer.

Then on February 20, 1992, Jack and Rebecca welcomed their son Ray Nicholson, who was also born in Los Angeles.

Ray is also an actor who starred in the 2022 film, Out of the Blue. He was also featured in the 2021 Amazon Prime Video show, Panic.

While Jack has never publicly acknowledged her as his child, he reportedly has a 28-year-old actress daughter named Tessa Gourin.

As reported by the Daily Beast, her mother is a former real estate agent in New York named Jennine Gourin.

Does Jack Nicholson have grandchildren?

The Shining star has two known grandchildren.

His daughter Jennifer has two sons named Sean and Duke Norfleet with her ex-husband, Mark Norfleet.

Sean was born on February 20, 1996, and is 26 years old.

24-year-old Duke was born in 1999 and has pursued an acting career. He appeared in the 2018 movie, Us.

When was Jack Nicholson married?

Jack was married to Sandra for six years.

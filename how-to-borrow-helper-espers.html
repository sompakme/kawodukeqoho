Borrowing Espers: A Guide for the Friendless (and the Friend-Full, Too!)

Ah, Espers. Those dazzling deities with devastating attacks and questionable fashion choices. But let's face it, building your dream team can be a grueling gacha grind. Enter the glorious world of borrowing Helper Espers from your friends, a practice as old as borrowing your buddy's gym clothes in high school (except hopefully less… questionable).

Step 1: Befriend People (or Pretend You Did)

This might seem counterintuitive, but hear me out. You wouldn't borrow a cup of sugar from your mortal enemy, right? So, go cultivate some friendships! Chat them up in the in-game chat, compliment their Espers (even if they're rocking a questionable Helga skin), and maybe even share some of your own gacha woes. Who knows, you might even make a real friend in the process!

Bonus Tip: If you're feeling particularly friendless, there are always online communities dedicated to Dislyte. Just be sure to avoid the shady characters offering "free" Espers – they might just end up stealing your Relic collection (and your self-respect).

Step 2: Navigate the Friend-Zone (It's Not What You Think)

Now that you've got a squad (or at least a few acquaintances), head to the Friends tab and tap the Assists button. This is your gateway to the land of borrowed brilliance.

Pro Tip: Don't be afraid to stalk (ahem, browse) your friends' Espers. Look for those high-level powerhouses with the skills to obliterate your enemies.

Step 3: Choose Wisely, Grasshopper

Remember, with great borrowed power comes great responsibility (and the potential for judgement). Don't just pick the flashiest Esper. Consider your battle strategy and team composition.

Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Roles: Do you need a healer to keep your team alive, a tank to soak up damage, or a damage dealer to go full Rambo?
  • Elements: Does your team synergy benefit from a specific element, or are you going for a rainbow (read: chaotic) approach?
  • Relics: Are your friend's Espers packing the heat with powerful relics, or are they sporting the equivalent of participation trophies?

Step 4: Reap the Rewards (and Maybe Send Thank-You Flowers)

Once you've chosen your champion, you're good to go! Borrow that Esper, conquer your enemies, and bask in the glory (and try not to forget who helped you achieve it).

Remember: A thoughtful message or even a simple "thanks, bro!" can go a long way in maintaining positive friend-zones and ensuring a steady supply of borrowed Espers.

So, there you have it! Borrowing Helper Espers: a simple solution to complex team-building woes. Now go forth, form your alliances, and conquer the world (or at least climb the leaderboards). Just remember, with great power comes great responsibility... and the potential to accidentally borrow your guild leader's Esper. Oops.

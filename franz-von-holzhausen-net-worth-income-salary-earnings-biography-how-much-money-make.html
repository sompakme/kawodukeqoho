Franz von Holzhausen (born May 10, 1968 in Simsbury, Connecticut) is an automobile designer who is currently in charge of design at Tesla Motors after resigning from his position as design director at Mazda North American Operations on July 31, 2008. He previously worked as design director at Mazda North American Operations. In 2008, he began designing the Tesla Model S, an electric luxury car that is still in production today.

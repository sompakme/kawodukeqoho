Lil Wayne Explains Celebrity Beach Bowl Incident

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  180 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Although we didn't have much information about why the scuffle happened yesterday, now footage has surfaced of Lil Wayne explaining why he was upset with the camera man following the game. Apparently the camera man pushed Weezy F.

"Because one of these camera dudes just pushed me just now, and I don't like to be touched by people I don't tell to touch me. So I'ma get out of the building 'cause if I touch I go to jail, 'cause they not the color of me, they not the same color I am, and you know how that go. But to them, fuck you," a visibly angry Wayne said.

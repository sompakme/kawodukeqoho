Rapper and TV host Bow Wow is looking at a career in WWE wrestling which he says has been his "life long childhood dream".

The 31-year-old shared tweet on Monday, February 8, confirming that he will be dropping his last album before focusing on other stuff, which includes the WWE.

Voicing his desire to be a wrestler, Bow Wow also said that the news might appear a bit crazy but he was ready for the next chapter.

He wrote:

"Now I know this might sound crazy... but after I drop my last album, I will focus on tv and film. And joining the @WWE. It's been a lifelong childhood dream to wrestle in the WWE."

In a follow-up tweet, the former 106 & Park TV host said he wanted to be in a tag team with the mask man Rey Mysterio and their tag team would be ready for any other duo.

His announcement led to a heated exchange with some WWE superstars ready to kick his a** in the ring while some were all about love as they welcomed him to the franchise.

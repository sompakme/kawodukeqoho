Although the broadcasting industry in Nigeria is still growing and improving, there are already over a hundred broadcasting channels in Nigeria. Below is a complete list of all the TV stations in Nigeria and their frequencies.

There are more than a hundred television stations in Nigeria. And all of them have been given broadcasting license by the National Broadcasting Commission (NBC).

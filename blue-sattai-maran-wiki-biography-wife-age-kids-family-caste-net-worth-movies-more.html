Blue Sattai Maran Career, Youtube Videos & Movies

Blue Sattai has not only worked as a Youtuber but earlier he was involved in many different works. He first at the age of 15 joined a film Distribution Company in Madurai and worked for a short time with his uncle.

Later Sattai getting knowledge of film distribution started his own film distribution company with his three brothers but didn’t succeed in it. Mr. Maran after it started a different business related to the film industry but didn’t do well in any of them.

He then become a part of the production team in Tamil and Kannada films and was a part of Ram Babu Production In Kannada films. He has worked in the Ram babu Productions team for more than 23 movies. In the year 2009 Maran was providing his service as an assistant manager of popular Tamil star. When he was serving this star, a popular film of this Tamil star was released at that time.

But that film was very bad and anyone can recommend that Tamil film as a worse movie. And Sattai was aware of the fact that this Tamil movie is worse and came to know that the senior manager from him has published good reviews about that movie in the newspaper by offering a good amount of money to news agencies.

He talked about this with the manager and asked him why he published false about that Tamil star movie for which he was serving as a manager. And his senior manager said that he will get edit it. But nothing changed and it made Maaran so furious that it was a bad film and these newspapers are doing this.

This was the moment that changed his mind to become a movie reviewer. So he got help from two of his family friend and created his Youtube Channel linked to the cinema.

The friend who helped him was a software professional and handled his all technical work and he himself become the host of his Youtube Channel Tamil Talkies. In the year 2012, he posted his first Youtube video after opening his small office in Vadapalani.

When Blue shot his first video he was very much nervous but got success in sharing his first-ever reviews for the movie Billa 2.

“When a director is talented, he can even make a fly the hero. But when the director has no matter, he can give an utter flop even with Ajith,” said Maran, in the first of several truth bombs that he dropped from his Youtube Channel video.

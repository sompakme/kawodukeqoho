Harry Kane 'over the moon' as he announces he is expecting his fourth child

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  414 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Tottenham star is already a father to two girls and a boy  

    Congratulations to Harry Kane, who is expecting his fourth child with his wife Kate Goodland. The England star announced the happy news via social media on Wednesday, stating he is "over the moon," to welcome a newborn into his clan.

    The Spurs captain and his wife Katie are already proud parents to three children: Ivy, four, Vivienne Jane, two, and Louis, six months – as well as the couple’s two Labradors, Brady and Wilson.

    The couple shared the news via Instagram, writing: "Over the moon to be expecting baby No.4! Coming soon…"

    Fans celebrated the news alongside the striker, taking to his comment sections to congratulate the couple. "He shoots, he scores!! Congratulations to you both," one wrote, while another said: "Congrats guys! Lovely news." A third noted: "Congrats out goat," and a fourth similarly penned: "Wonderful news."

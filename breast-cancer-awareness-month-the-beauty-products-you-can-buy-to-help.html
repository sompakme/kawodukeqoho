Breast Cancer Awareness Month: the beauty products you can buy to help

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  497 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

    This October is Breast Cancer Awareness Month, and there are so many great products you can buy to help support the cause.

    We've rounded up some of our favourite beauty must-haves...

    Elemis Breast Cancer Care Face & Body Wellbeing Collection

    Give yourself a night off and enjoy some well-deserved pampering with this gorgeous kit, that has four luxurious skincare products for that spa-at-home feeling. You'll be left feeling calm, nourished and revitalised –the perfect way to kick off the winter months!

    £55, available at beautyexpert.com 

    ghd Platinum Electric Styler in 'Electric Pink'

    These straighteners use innovative tri-zone® technology to make sure you're styling your hair at optimum temperature, leaving you with healthy, strong hair that has an enhanced shine. The best bit? For every styler bought, ghd will donate £10 to Breast Cancer Now.

    £165, available at hqhair.com 

    Morgan Taylor Fight Like A Lady Collection

    Keep your manicure looking flawless while donating to a great cause with this four-piece collection, which includes handy travel-sized versions of the brand's polishes  and 10 per cent of sales will be donated to Future Dreams Charity, which raises money for women and families living with breast cancer.

    OPI 3 Piece Breast Cancer Care Collection

    This three-piece set includes a velvety smooth hand and body lotion with luscious scents of peony and poppy, as well as two of the brand's iconic nail polishes in candy pink and glittering grey. You'll be supporting a worthy cause – 100 per cent of the sale price will be paid to Breast Cancer Care.

    Kiss Air Candles in Pink Roses

    A great candle can make all the difference for a cosy night in, and we're loving this candles scent's floral notes including lilac, gardenia, carnation and hyacinth for a luscious fragrance. Handmade in Cambridgeshire using natural soy wax, this limited edition will be donating £1.50 from every candle sold to the Addenbrooke's Breast Cancer Appeal.

    Lulu's Time Bomb Revealing Body Cleanser 200ml

    Up your skincare game with this exfoliator that has a small amount of polishing crystals and exfoliating beads to gently cleanse your skin and leave it as radiant and smooth-looking as ever. Add it to your beauty kit to help a great cause, with all proceeds from sales going to Breast Cancer Care.

    Philip Kingsley No Scent, No Colour Shampoo & Conditioner

    Created specifically to help men and women undergoing treatment for cancer, this shampoo and conditioner's formula's include no parabens, sulfates or scents, making them ideal for gentle yet effective cleansing. Plus they will help a great cause, with 50p from every 250ml bottle will be donated to Look Good Feel Better, a charity dedicated to improving the wellbeing and self-confidence of people undergoing treatment for cancer.

    invisibobble Pink Heroes Edition

    The go-to for a quick hair fix, invisibobble have released a new limited edition of their iconic hair ties to show their support for breast cancer awareness, with a rose gold charm gracing the metallic pink bobble, engraved with the pink ribbon.

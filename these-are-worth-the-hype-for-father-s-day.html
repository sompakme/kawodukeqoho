Lululemon Father’s Day 2024

ABC Classic-Fit 5 Pocket Pant Warpstreme

First up: the ABC Classic-Fit 5 Pocket Warpstreme ($128), which comes in a slew of stylish and classic colors. It honestly looks like a well-fitted pair of jeans, but without any of the discomfort you can sometimes get with denim. It was designed with a four-way stretch fabric that keeps its shape, while also being sweat-wicking and breathable (hence its place on the golf course!). It also has a streamlined fit that has the brand’s proprietary technology to prevent bunching in the crotch. It’s no wonder one recent shopper called them the “most comfortable trousers ever,” adding that that these have a great fit. Plus, you can’t deny the size range (26 to 48, with lengths ranging from 28″ to 37″) is incredible.

ABC Slim-Fit Trouser Warpstreme

Another solid option: the ABC Slim-Fit Pant Warpstreme (also $128), which has a slim fit for a slightly more tailored style. It’s also made using fabric that won’t wrinkle, which is a huge A-plus in our book. These pants are available in a nice selection of colors including Taupetastic, Raw Linen, and Black, this pair has received equally stellar reviews from shoppers, with one calling them the “most comfortable, best looking pants I’ve ever owned.” Sizes for these pants range from 28 to 46, with 28″, 30″, 32″, 34″, and 37″ lengths.

ABC Slim-Fit Trouser Stretch Cotton VersaTwill

Time to trade in those chinos for something a lot more comfortable! Lululemon’s ABC Slim-Fit Trouser Stretch Cotton VersaTwill is described as “how chinos should feel.” There’s plenty of stretch and extra room in the crotch, making it super easy to move around in.

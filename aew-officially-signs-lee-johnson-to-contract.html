AEW Officially Signs Lee Johnson To Contract

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  231 words  |  Larita Shotwell

AEW Dynamite was a big night for Lee Johnson. He picked up the win in his tag team match with Cody Rhodes against Peter Avalon and Cezar Bononi. Tony Khan also had big news for him after the match.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite from this week.

Lee Johnson is recent inductee into the Nightmare Family. He is also now officially signed with All Elite Wrestling. Tony Khan broke the news after Johnson’s match that he has signed a full-time deal with the company.

In April most of the roster couldn’t travel & we needed local talent for #AEWDynamite. @CodyRhodes recommended his pupil @BigShottyLee. Lee came & worked so hard. I was blown away & kept booking him. With Cody’s guidance Lee got his 1st win tonight! Congrats Lee now it’s official

Lee Johnson will have some great minds around him to assist his career. This is definitely an exciting development for Lee Johnson as he continues into 2021. Now Johnson is AEW.

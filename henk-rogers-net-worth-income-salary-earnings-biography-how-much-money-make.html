Henk Rogers (born December 24, 1953) is a video game creator and entrepreneur who works in the video game industry. Among his many accomplishments are the creation of Japan’s first major turn-based role-playing video game, The Black Onyx, obtaining the rights to distribute Tetris on video game consoles where the game gained popularity, and being the founder of The Tetris Company, which holds a licence to the Tetris trademark.

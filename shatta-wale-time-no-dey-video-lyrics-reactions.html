Shatta Wale - Time No Dey: video, lyrics, reactions

Ghanaian producer and musician Shatta Wale recently dropped a new single. It is an excellent piece with captivating beats and a beautiful video to match. The jam by Shatta Wale - Time No Dey is entertaining to listen to and watch.

Fans have shown much love to the artist and the fact that the video quality is impressive. The video was directed by PKMI and produced by Kofi Abban.

Madewell Ariana Midi Dress

Now that I can finally put away my puffer coats and start shopping for dresses, the first one I plan on adding to my cart is Madewell’s Ariana Midi Dress. It comes in a soft blue with an all-over ditsy floral pattern, and sizes range from 00 to 16. The dress features a sleeveless V-neckline and shirring under the bust. It has a body-skimming fit that falls at the mid-calf depending on your height and doesn’t have a zipper, so you can just pull it on and go. I’d style it with a matching light blue cardigan on chillier days with white sneakers or sandals like Steve Madden’s Santana Slide Sandal.

Longchamp Le Pliage Cosmetics Case

I’ve always been a fan of Longchamp’s Le Pliage Tote bags, so when I saw this cute little Le Pliage Cosmetics Case, I couldn’t check out my cart fast enough in fear that it would sell out. Made out of a water-resistant nylon fabric, it comes in black, green, and blue, and has a contrasting brown leather top handle with a matching snap-flap closure (plus a secondary zipper closure for extra security). The small but mighty size is perfect for carrying your beauty essentials for on-the-go.

L'Occitane Shea Mani and Pedi Set

During the winter months, I usually give my nails a break from manicures, but now that the weather is starting to warm up, they’re definitley in need of a little TLC. This is why I plan on picking up L'Occitane’s Shea Mani and Pedi Set, which includes my favorite nourishing cuticle oil, and a foot and hand cream that moisturizes and softens rough, dry skin. It also comes with a nail file and a cuticle pusher to shape and prep nails for polish — all for $33. Now you can get a salon-quality manicure and pedicure in the comfort of your home.

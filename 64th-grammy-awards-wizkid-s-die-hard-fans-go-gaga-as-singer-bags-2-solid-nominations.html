64th Grammy Awards: Wizkids Die-Hard Fans Go Gaga As Singer Bags 2 Solid Nominations

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  429 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Nigerian singer Wizkid continues to give his fans something to talk and boast about when it comes to his career
  • The musician's body of work has been listed in two categories for the prestigious 64th Grammy Awards
  • The Ojuelegba crooner was listed alongside some Nigerian artistes and singers from other parts of the world

Nigerian singer Wizkid made the 64th Grammy Awards' list under the category of the best global music performance and the best global music album, Variety reports.

Wizkid's music Essence would be battling against Angelique Kidjo and Burna Boy's Do Yourself, Femi Kuti's PÀ PÁ PÀ, Yo-Yo Ma and Angelique Kidjo Blewu, and Arooj Aftab Mohabbat, under the best global music performance category.

Meanwhile, singer Tems was also mentioned as her music with Wizkid was the one nominated.

Wizkid wins three AFRIMA awards

Nigerian superstar Wizkid bagged three awards at the eighth edition of the All Africa Music Awards (AFRIMA).

Wizkid beat others to emerge as the artiste of the year. His music, Essence, featuring Tems, won song of the year and best African collaboration.

The Essence crooner battled with artistes from Nigeria like Davido, Adekunle Gold, Ladipoe, among others to emerge the winner. Artistes from other countries include Taycofficel from Cameroon, Focalistic from South Africa, and many others.

 Posted on September 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  457 words  |  Martina Birk

AHEAD of her boxing debut, reality TV star Natalie Nunn has been making the most of London's nightlife.

Nunn is set to face fellow Baddies star Tommie Lee on the undercard at Floyd Mayweather and Aaron Chalmers's bout in London's O2 Arena on February 25.

And with the fight just days away, Nunn and her friends hit the town on Monday night.

Her Instagram story lit up throughout the evening, starting at a nightclub where, standing beside her husband Jacob Payne, she warned "you're gonna feel it bi**h" - presumably in reference to Lee.

She captioned the clip: "We all at dinner and where r u not here not invited YOU MESSED UR BAG UP."

Joined by fellow Baddies star Rollie Pollie, Nunn and her friends and family were "having a ball," enjoying a luxury meal before heading out clubbing once again.

Captioning a clip of her dancing: "Outside hahahaha no wig on having a ball cause I'm not insecure hahahahah," she commented "We lit london" and that "Baddies west runs it."

Partying aside, the tension has been rising all week as the fight approaches.

A scrap broke out at a press conference between the two on Monday, ending in the two having to be pulled apart with Lee escorted out of the room.

On Wednesday, Lee walked out of a press conference when Nunn was asked how the two were friends and enemies at the same time, prompting Nunn to call her opponent out on stage.

She said: "I mean everybody wants to be the best Bad Girl.

"I'm at the top. Look at her walking out. Look at her leaving. I understand. It's hard to sit up here with me. I get it."

Later calling out: "Where are you going Tommie? Come back! We love you!"

That day, Nunn also predicted that she's going to "knock that bit***s s**t loose.

And when it came to discussing the fight, she believes Tommie is in over her head, not realizing the type of "athlete" she agreed to face.

She said: "Well, first and foremost I'm all about women empowerment too.

"Before this TV stuff, I was an athlete. I was the number one soccer player in the country out of high school.

"And I have two degrees from USC. So ain't nobody over here dumb.

"So this is going to be a competition in the ring. Tommy, I don't think she knows what she got herself into cause I'm a real athlete.

"And this ain't street fighting. She can talk about all her mugshots all day.

Top Nigerian singer David Adeleke ‘Davido’ might not have expected to get the reaction he got from a fan whose comment he replied to.

An X (Twitter) user, Abazz, announced on the app that he had made a picture frame of the comment the music star dropped on his page.

Prior to that tweet, Abazz had posted about Davido not tweeting for about three days and how he missed him. The fan also asked about the singer’s well-being.

"One thing about these fans is that they tend to idolize these celebrities. And stop seeing them like mare humans like themselves. I mean, if it makes him happy, then there’s no issue. At a point in his life, he’ll get over it."

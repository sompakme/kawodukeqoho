Ravens HC John Harbaugh goes viral for sarcastic interview with female reporter

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  218 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Baltimore Ravens head coach John Harbaugh went viral Sunday night after delivering a tasteless, sarcastic interview to NBC reporter Melissa Stark during his team's Super Wild Card Weekend match against the Cincinnati Bengals at Paycor Stadium.

The interview happened in between the first and second quarter, when Baltimore trailed Cincinnati by three points.

Bengals quarterback Joe Burrow would go on to make it 9-0 in favor of the home side on the first play of the second quarter.

Harbaugh, 60, was understandably upset, but that's no reason to be rude to a person trying to do their job.

Stark, 49, couldn't even get her questions out before Harbaugh interrupted her with short, dry answers.

Ravens closing in on Bengals

Ravens managed to score a touchdown in the second quarter to make it a two-point game.

They could have had a second one, but settled for a field goal on 4th & GOAL to take a 10-9 lead into halftime.

Harbaugh will likely be fuming in the locker room knowing that his team could have been up by at least four more points.

As of this publication, Burrow has thrown for 122 yards and a touchdown on 14-for-19 completed passes.

Ravens quarterback Tyler Huntley played the entire first half despite Harbaugh claiming he'd share snaps with Anthony Brown.

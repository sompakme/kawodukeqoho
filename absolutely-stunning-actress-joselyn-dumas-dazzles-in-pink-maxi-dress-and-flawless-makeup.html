Award-winning actress Joselyn Dumas has partnered with top Ghanaian fashion brand Pistis Ghana to be their celebrity muse for the New Year collection. She showed that she is ready for what 2024 has to offer.

The fashion icon looked gorgeous in a pink ruffled sleeves maxi dress with unique detailing for bold and stylish women.

She looked charming in flawless makeup and a lustrous frontal hairstyle that elevated her look. The 43-year-old wore simple drop earrings and bracelets as she posed on the polished wooden staircase.

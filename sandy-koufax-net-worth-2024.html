Sandy Koufax’s Baseball Career

Sandy Koufax’s net worth is largely attributed to his successful career in baseball. He played for the Brooklyn/Los Angeles Dodgers from 1955 to 1966. His exceptional pitching skills earned him a place in the Baseball Hall of Fame in 1972.

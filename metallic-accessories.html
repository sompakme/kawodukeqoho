Bling It On: Metallic Accessories

Saint Laurent

This poppy-hued metallic fold over clutch ($990; net-a-porter.com) is a standout piece. Carry it to anchor an all-black or white monochromatic look.

Marc by Marc Jacobs

A rosy metallic is fit for springtime. Add this low wedge sandal ($298; nordstrom.com) to your stash of shoes to modernize your collection.

Pono

Incorporate small doses of metallic into your daywear with this iridescent chain link necklace ($325; ponobyjoangoodman.com).

Givenchy

These sneakers ($650; net-a-porter.com) steer clear of casual. Take your daytime look to the next level with Givenchy's luminous slide-on sneakers.

Forever 21

Dress up a white tee and jeans with these high-shine, geometric earrings ($4; forever21.com).

Avec Moderation

Who says metallic washes are limited to golds and silvers? This bright, colorful sandal ($355; shopbop.com) outshines the rest.

Zilla

Accessorize with Zilla's glossy film clutch ($245; modaoperandi.com) to spruce up any look.

BCBG

Treat this molten slide ($59; bcbg.com) as your go-to shoe of the season. Wear it with everything to infuse each and every look with a bit of shine and forget about that "you can't mix metals" myth.

Why Kim Kardashian is about to enter India's Big Brother house

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  157 words  |  Martina Birk

Kim Kardashian will enter India's Big Brother house this weekend. Photo/Getty

Kim Kardashian is heading to the Big Brother house to guest star in the Indian version of the reality TV show.

Kardashian will enter the house on Saturday and stay for an unspecified length of time, with cameras following her every move as she learns to live with the 12 other contestants.

The series, titled Bigg Boss, follows a number of local celebrities during a 15-week period and during every season, an international star appears in the house.

In 2010, former Baywatch actress Pamela Anderson stayed for three days.

Kardashian, who will visit India for the first time this week following her visit to Australia, confirmed her appearance in a statement in Hindi, which translates as: "Hello India, I am Kim Kardashian and I am coming to the Bigg Boss house."

The show is hosted by Bollywood star Salman Khan.
